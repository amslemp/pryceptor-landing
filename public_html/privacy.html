Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 7, 2025

FERPA Compliance

Pryceptor is committed to full compliance with the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA). We do not collect or store personally identifiable information (PII) about students without explicit institutional authorization. All institutional data provided to us is anonymized or de-identified before analysis, unless otherwise contractually secured in accordance with FERPA guidelines.

GDPR Compliance

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), we respect your data rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). You may request access, correction, or deletion of any data we store related to your interaction with our platform by contacting us at privacy@pryceptor.com.

Data We Collect

  • Basic contact form submissions (name, email, message)
  • Anonymized website analytics via Google Analytics
  • No student records, academic performance data, or institutional data are collected through this website unless explicitly submitted via contact form

Cookies

We use cookies to provide site analytics and enhance user experience. By continuing to use our site, you consent to our use of cookies. You may opt out at any time using the cookie settings provided in the banner.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, email us at privacy@pryceptor.com.
